01. Overview

At RANGEDOPE, we prioritize the operational security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the RANGEDOPE Precision Ballistics System. We operate under a ZERO-RETENTION default for non-essential field data.

02. Information We Collect

To provide precision ballistic calculations, the application accesses specific hardware telemetry. This includes environmental sensor data, GPS coordinates WHEN IN USE ONLY, and device orientation.

  • target Atmospheric Data: Barometric pressure, humidity, and temperature retrieved from local sensors or nearby stations.
  • target Ballistic Profiles: Custom projectile data and rifle configurations stored locally on your device.
  • target Subscription Status: Managed via REVENUECAT to verify active license status.

03. Data Security

Operational security is paramount. All ballistic solutions and terrain maps are stored using AES-256 encryption. We do not transmit your precise firing positions or mission logs to our central servers unless manually initiated for backup purposes.

CRITICAL NOTICE

Deletion of the application results in the immediate purging of all locally stored ballistic profiles. We recommend using the 'Export Dope' feature before hardware decommissioning.
