Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 3, 2025

Overview

RangeDOPE ("the App") is a long-range ballistic solver and hunt planning tool for iOS. This policy describes what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We built this app for hunters and long-range shooters who care about their privacy — so we have kept data collection to the bare minimum required to deliver core functionality.

Information We Collect

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location (When In Use) for the following purposes:

  • Auto-filling the shooter position (latitude, longitude, elevation) in the Shot Plan screen
  • Fetching local atmospheric data for ballistic corrections (temperature, pressure, humidity)

Your location is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties. We do not collect or store your location history. Location access is optional — you may enter coordinates manually.

Hunt Waypoints

Waypoints (sightings, stands, glassing points, etc.) and shot plan data you enter are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. This data is never uploaded to any server and is not accessible to us.

Purchases & Subscriptions

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements. RevenueCat may collect a pseudonymous device identifier for entitlement verification. No payment information is ever processed by us directly. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy for details on payment data handling.

Map Tiles

The App loads topographic and satellite map tiles from third-party services including the USGS National Map and ESRI World Imagery. These requests are standard tile fetches that may include your approximate viewing area (bounding box), consistent with any other mapping application. We do not control or store this data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account information
  • We do not track your location in the background
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or sell data to advertisers
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as described above (RevenueCat, Apple)
  • We do not use third-party analytics (no Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, etc.)

Data Retention & Deletion

All app data (rifle profiles, shot plans, waypoints) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the App from your device removes all locally stored data permanently. RevenueCat retains purchase records to restore entitlements; contact RevenueCat directly for deletion requests related to their records.

Children

RangeDOPE is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is rated 17+ on the App Store.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
